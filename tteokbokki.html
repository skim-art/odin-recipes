This is tteokbokki. It is a Korean dish usually eaten as a snack made with rice cakes, fish cakes, onions, and an egg to top it all off. It is very delicious!

Warning: for those of mild preferences, tteokbokki can be EXTREMELYspicy! Make sure to not eat the spiciest version the first time you try this dish. This is my favorite!

Ingredients

  • Rice cakes
  • Red pepper sauce
  • Water
